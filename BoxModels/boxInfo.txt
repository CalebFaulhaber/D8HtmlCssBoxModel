flexBox:
1. Create container.
2. display: flex;(or whatever type of box you want)
   - display: flex; must be in every new container.
3. add inner container hight and width
4. add align-items:(wherever you want);
   -- or --
   add justify-content:(wherever you want);
5. align-items/justify-content: 
   space-between/space-around/space-evenly (if needed)


- style="align-items:center 
    - aligns from top to bottom

- style='justify-content: center
    - centers from left to right

- style="justify-content: space-evenly
    - adds an eqeal space between each box.
